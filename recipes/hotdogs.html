Baked Chili Hot Dog Recipe

A highly nutricious meal packed with processed meats and cheese

Hot dogs in buns are topped with HORMELÂ® Chili with Beans and shredded Cheddar cheese then baked until piping hot.

Only the finest hot dog weiners should be used when creating this beautiful meal

Ingredients

  • 6 hot dogs
  • 6 split hot dogs buns
  • 1 (15 ounce) can HORMEL® Chili With Beans
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Recipe instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease 9 x 13-inch baking dish.
  2. Heat hot dogs according to package directions. Arrange hot dog buns in baking dish, cut side up. Place hot dogs in buns. Top with chili. Sprinkle with cheese. Tent pan with foil.
  3. Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until hot and cheese is melted.
